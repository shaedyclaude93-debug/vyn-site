Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

At VYN AI Agent ("VYN", "we", "us", or "our"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and business details you provide
  • Platform credentials: API keys and OAuth tokens for connected platforms (stored locally on your device)
  • Business data: Financial records, receipts, invoices, and tax information you enter
  • Social media data: Posts, follower counts, and engagement metrics from connected platforms
  • Usage data: How you interact with the VYN dashboard

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the VYN AI Agent service
  • Generate AI-powered insights, briefings, and recommendations
  • Sync data between connected third-party platforms
  • Calculate tax estimates, Safe to Spend amounts, and business health scores
  • Send WhatsApp notifications and briefings you have configured
  • Improve the VYN service

3. Data Storage

VYN AI Agent stores your data primarily on your local device. API keys, financial records, and personal data are stored in configuration files on your own machine — not on our servers. You maintain full control of your data at all times.

4. Third-Party Platforms

VYN integrates with third-party platforms including TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Shopify, Stripe, and Xero. When you connect these platforms:

  • We access only the data necessary to provide VYN's features
  • Your credentials are stored locally on your device
  • Each platform's own privacy policy also applies to data shared through their API
  • We do not sell your platform data to third parties

5. TikTok Integration

When you connect TikTok to VYN, we access your TikTok account data (including profile information, follower counts, and content metrics) solely to display analytics within your VYN dashboard and to schedule/publish content on your behalf. We do not share your TikTok data with any third parties. You can disconnect TikTok at any time through the Connect tab.

6. AI Processing

VYN uses AI models (including Anthropic Claude and xAI Grok) to generate insights, post drafts, and briefings. Data sent to these AI services is subject to their respective privacy policies. We minimise the personal data included in AI prompts where possible.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Local-first data storage (your data stays on your device)
  • Encrypted API key storage
  • No centralised database of user financial information

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With connected platforms as required to provide the integration service
  • If required by Australian law or valid legal process

9. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

10. Children's Privacy

VYN is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes. Continued use of VYN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
